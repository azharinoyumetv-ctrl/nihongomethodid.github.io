About

Nihongo Method ID is an educational platform that automates the creation of Japanese language learning content using artificial intelligence. The platform streamlines lesson generation, narration, video production, and publishing workflows for educational purposes.

Features

  • AI-assisted lesson generation
  • Japanese vocabulary and grammar content
  • Automated voice generation
  • Automated educational video production
  • Content publishing workflow integration
  • Educational content management

TikTok Integration

Nihongo Method ID integrates with TikTok to publish educational Japanese learning videos created within the platform. Users can prepare content and publish it directly through the supported TikTok APIs after authorization.
